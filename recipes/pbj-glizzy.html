Peanut butter & Jelly Glizzy

The peanut butter and jelly glizzy combines canadian peanut butter and jelly sandwhiches with american hot dogs

Ingredients

  • Hot dog bun
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly or jam (house choice)
  • Banana

Steps

  1. Cut into the bun if not pre-cut
  2. Spread the hot dog bun wide open
  3. Smear peanut butter and jelly on opposite sides of the glizzy
  4. Peel banana
  5. Place peeled banana betweeen the peanut butter and jam/jelly

Enjoy your meal!

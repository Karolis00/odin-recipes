Croissant Dog

The croissant dog combines the american hot dog and the french croissant

Ingredients

  • Croissant
  • Hot dog
  • Ketchup (optional)

Steps

  1. Toast the croissant
  2. Slice it vertically
  3. Place a hot dog within the cut
  4. You may garnish with ketchup (but not mustard)

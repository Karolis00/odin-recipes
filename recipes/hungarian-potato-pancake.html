Hungarian Potato Pancakes

The hungarian potato pancakes combine polish potato pancakes and hungarian goulash

Ingredients

For the hungarian goulash

  • 700 grams pork shoulder or neck
  • 40 grams flour
  • 1 onion
  • cup o' water
  • 1 teaspoon paprika powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1.5 teaspoon salt

for the polish potato pancakes

  • 1000 grams potatoes
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 onion (not optional)
  • 1 garlic clove (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Prepare the goulash

    1. Cut meat to pieces
    2. Make spice mix
    3. Marinate cut meat with spice mix
    4. Chop and fry the onion
    5. Add meat
    6. Add water, cover pan, low fire
    7. After hour you may add flour to thicken the goulash if necessary
    8. Place goulash on pancakes
  2. Prepare the pancakes
    1. Grate peeled potatoes
    2. Drain grated peeled potatoes, but collect the precious starch
    3. Mix drained grated peeled potatoes with eggs, onion and maybe garlic
    4. Add potato starch
    5. Cook the mixed drained grated peeled potatoes in pancake form
    6. Place the potato pancake under the goulash
